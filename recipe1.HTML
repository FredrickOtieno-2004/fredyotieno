Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and mix in grated cheeses and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water, and immediately add to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese and black pepper.

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 650 | Protein: 30g | Carbs: 75g | Fat: 25g
